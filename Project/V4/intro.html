Introduction to No Man's Sky

No Man's Sky is a video game available for most consoles and personal computers. At its essence, No Man's Sky is a game about exploration and disovery. The developers wanted to provide as big a sandbox as they could for players to travel through and make their own.


The game features activities such as:

  • Space Travel
  • Dog Fights
  • Trading
  • Multiplayer
  • Fauna & Flora Cataloguing
  • Base Building
  • And Much More
